        \item Let $f(x_0,x) = \theta(x_0-\abs{x})$. We verify that $f_a(x_0,x) = e^{-ax_0}f(x_0,x)$ converges to $f(x_0,x)$ in $\mathcal S^\prime$ as $a\to 0^+$. Let $\varphi\in \mathcal S$ be any test function. Then
